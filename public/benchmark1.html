Puzzle Knight
Introduction
Our game “Puzzle Knight” is a fantasy-themed top-down RPG game where our main character will have to solve puzzles and fight enemies to reach his goal. The game will make use of sprites, tilemaps, collision detection, AI, and camera-panning.
Technology
Puzzle Knight will be made using Wolfie-2D, a 2D game engine created by Richard McKenna. DirectX is being used for rendering, and our pixel art will be made with Piskel. Music and sound will be made with Audacity and GarageBand.
Backstory
The Puzzle Knight is a brave and intelligent warrior, sworn by oath to protect the kingdom’s royal family. While our hero was off on an important mission, dragons launched an assault on the kingdom and escaped with the royal princess. When our hero returned from his mission and heard what happened, he sprung into action. Puzzle Knight made his way to the entrance of the Lair of Dragons, determined to bring the princess to safety...
Objective
The objective of the game is to locate the boss and slay it. The player will need to explore the map and complete puzzles in order to acquire upgrades to their equipment and skills necessary to defeat the boss.
Gameplay
  • Traversing the map is a large part of the game as the map itself acts as a puzzle.
  • Interacting with objects like chests/doors may require the completion of a puzzle.
  • Enemies like goblins/skeletons are located throughout the map, and the player will use a variety of weapons(knives/swords/bows) to defeat them
  • Defeating enemies awards XP, which levels the player and awards skill points
  • Skill points can be spent at the skill tree to provide a form of progression that persists across the levels.
Controls
  • Move UP: W
  • Move LEFT: A
  • Move DOWN: S
  • Move LEFT: D
  • Attack: LEFT-CLICK
  • Interact: RIGHT-CLICK
  • Select Item: NUMPAD

Graphical User Interface
How we plan to layout the UI for our many different scenes:
  • Splash Screen: We plan to show our logo, a start mechanism, and a brief show depicting the backstory of the game. This can be skipped.
  • Main Menu: Player can choose to start the game, select a level, learn more about the game, and view the controls.
  • Level Select: Player can choose which level to start on. Levels will be disabled/enabled according to player progression.
  • In-game: How the GUI will look in-game when the player is playing.
  • Controls: Tells the player how to play the game
  • Help: Displays the game's backstory, cheat codes, and developers.
